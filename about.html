Ten years of success and continuous growth

A HIGHLY AUTOMATION FACTORY EQUIPPED WITH THE MOST ADVANCED TECHNOLOGIES IN THE SECTOR. A TEAM OF ENGINEERS AND OPERATIONAL TECHNICIANS WITH HIGH SKILLS AND EXPERIENCE.

OUR MISSION: TO PROVIDE
SOLAR ENERGY IN “EVERYDAY”
APPLICATIONS

Photovoltaic panels for customized solutions off-grid and on-grid, ultralight, very thin, flexible, high performance, designed and made in Italy.

Since its foundation ten years ago, Powertrac has placed constant research on materials, technologies, production processes and applications at the center of its life, collaborating with Italian and foreign companies, universities and research centres.

Powertrac, with technologically cutting-edge solutions, seeks to meet the ever-increasing demand for photovoltaic panels with a minimal and hi-tech design, capable of combining aesthetic cleanliness with the technical needs of energy autonomy obtained from renewable sources.

Research and development

Powertrac uses the best technologies currently available, also thanks to continuous research and development activities. Thanks to its passion for innovation and valuable collaborations, Powertrac has completed and continues to develop important research projects.
